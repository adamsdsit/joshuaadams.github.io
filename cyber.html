Cybersecurity Fundamentals

Instructor: Dr. Joshua Adams

Term: Fall 2025

Course Description

Introduction to core cybersecurity principles including CIA triad, risk management, encryption, ethical hacking, and cyber law. Hands-on labs using Wireshark, Metasploit, and cloud-based security tools.

Learning Objectives

  • Analyze threats and vulnerabilities across network layers
  • Apply cryptographic principles to secure communications
  • Use cybersecurity tools in lab-based exercises
  • Design and assess risk mitigation strategies

Assessment

  • Labs & Reports – 30%
  • Midterm Exam – 25%
  • Capstone Project – 25%
  • Participation & Discussions – 20%
